Who I Am

HOWDY, I’M A FRONT-END DESIGNER AND DEVELOPER BASED IN THE BAY AREA.

User experience is the first and always the most important principle in my mind while designing. I also have worked as a QA. Creating enjoyable experience is what motives me to be a designer. I’m excited to work with other designers, engineers and stakeholders to make meaningful products and experiences!
