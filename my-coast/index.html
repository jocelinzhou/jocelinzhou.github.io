My Coast

Overview

This project aims to create a campaign for a nonprofit organization, spurring people to make a positive difference either in their life or in the world around them.

Approach

For this project, I create a new brand and the set of brand guidelines for this campaign organization; a paired of graphic advertisements, repurposable to a variety of media, and environmental marketing; a microsite mockup devoted to this specific campaign, which contains the community section for those involved and an interactive element to get viewers involved.

Peoblems

Approximately 1.4 billion pounds of trash per year enters the ocean. Much of it ends up on our beaches washed in with the waves and tides, some sinks, some is eaten by marine animals mistaking it for food. Marine litter broadly impact on enviroment, human and socioeconomic.

Research Infographic

Solution

Creating a campaign for a nonprofit organization, my coast, to spur residents to make a positive environmental change in the California coastline.

Brand Guideline

My coast aims to transmit the beauty of nature to the audiences, and encourage them to contutribute to coast clean activities. Therefore, I use the graduated blue and sand sand color as the logo. And the brand guidelines illustrated the logo usage, type style, color palette.
